Comparison with Existing Methods

We compare our approach against five state-of-the-art image-based text-to-SVG generation methods: CLIPDraw, DiffSketcher, VectorFusion, SVGDreamer, and T2V-NPR.

For language-based methods, we only send plain text prompts to the LLMs without any additional instructions (e.g., prompt expansion, visual rectification, in-context examples, etc.) and present the results below. We compare three different state-of-the-art LLMs: Claude Sonnet, GPT-4o, and GPT-o1-mini for evaluation.
